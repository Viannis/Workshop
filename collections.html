Why Web Development?

Knowingly or Unknowingly everyone around the world with mobile phones, pcs, etc... are tempted to view atleast a single website. Though desktop as well as mobile applications can replace them, due to lack of storage some are used as applications and rest are viewed as web pages.

According to Netcraft's January 2019 Web Server Survey there are more than 1 billion sites active at the current.

Knowing the exact needs of the people and targeting them via websites, benifits the upcoming engineers in terms of their career.
